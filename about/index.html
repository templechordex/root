Anji Teraoka

About

Anji Teraoka is a Japanese home recording musician creating lo-fi and psychedelic music.

Currently developing interactive 3D web music experiences using Three.js and Web Audio API.

Influenced by The Beatles, Tame Impala, Stereolab, and R. Stevie Moore.

Active in the 2020s and currently working on new music.
