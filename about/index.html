PSYCHEDELIC / DREAMY / POP / ROCK'N'ROLL

Anji Teraoka

Anji Teraoka is a solo music project creating Japanese-language psychedelic, dreamy, pop, and rock'n'roll music through kaleidoscopic, shifting sound. Working primarily through home recording, the songs explore everyday life and inner worlds.

Music

Songwriting, recording, and mixing are handled as part of the same independent production process.

Influenced by The Beatles, Tame Impala, Stereolab, and R. Stevie Moore.

Music + Web

Anji Teraoka is also developing interactive experiences that let listeners change how a song is heard in the browser, connecting music with responsive web-based expression.
